Painting, Quilling, & Craft classes by a professional artist with over 20 years of experience in art teaching.

Classes are Designed for total beginners to advanced level.

Introduction to sketching and colouring. Basic guidance on scale, tone, composition and perspective and experiment with all mediums of Art.

This course provides the tools to transfer a perceptional experience in pencil & colors to paper & canvas.

Classes for all age groups: toddlers, kids, teens to adults.

You can join Panache Art Classes at my studio at Jersey City or I can come to your location for individual or small groups.

Clases are available on weekdays and weekends which you can customize as per your availability & convenience.

Very reasonable changes, group discount for group of 3 or more kids

Wild Wings (age 2-3)
Fun filled class for toddlers

In this super exciting class students will

  • Learn with a wide variety of artistic media.
  • Begin to recognize art elements, such as line, shapes, color, and texture.
  • Enhance fine and gross motor skills.
  • Have a LOT of fun..fun...and fun...
  • *Class will be held once a week basis.
  • **If you have group of 3 or more kids, I can come to your location to take the class.

  • *Class will be held once a week basis.
  • **If you have group of 3 or more kids, I can come to your location to take the class.

Young Picasso (age 5-12)

Young artists develop ideas, explore new material and challenge their imaginations by exploring the work and techniques used by contemporary artists, while creating their own unique paintings and collages.

  • Experiment with different artistic mediums.
  • Learn a variety of techniques and forms.
  • Build confidence in expressing yourself artistically.
  • Watch your skills develop in a supportive and open environment.
  • Be guided based on their personal artistic level, interests and needs.
  • Create artwork you can be proud of.
  • Have a LOT of fun!.
  • Create many finished paitings to celebrate and hang in your home. Create artwork you can be proud of.
  • *Class will held once a week or fixed sessions. It is flexible based on your need.
  • **If you have group of 3 or more kids, I can come to your location to take the class.

Express yourself (age 13-17)

In this class your teen will learn and experience a variety of art mediums. Allow their inner artist to shine through as your teen works with paints, drawing tools, and more. In these classes, our students will:

  • Explore subject matter such as realism, abstraction, color fields, portraiture, wildlife, landscape, and more.
  • Enjoy the creative exploration of painting, collage with a unique mix of materials.
  • Learn artistic techniques like composition, 3-dimensionality, shading, painting with color and texture, abstraction, realism, and more.
  • Learn about art history’s influences on today’s modern artists.
  • Be guided based on their personal artistic level, interests and needs.
  • Create many finished paitings to celebrate and hang in your home. Create artwork you can be proud of.
  • *Class will held once a week or fixed sessions. It is flexible based on your need.
  • **If you have group of 3 or more kids, I can come to your location to take the class.

    What is quilling?

    Quilling is a beautiful paper-craft technique used to make coiled embellishments like flowers, hearts, butterflies, scrolls, marquise and more from colorful strips of paper for card making, scrapbooking or even jewelry!.
